Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2024

Welcome to Norwood Cafe and Restaurant's privacy policy. This document outlines the ways in which we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you visit our website hosted on Vercel.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information when you:

  • Make a reservation
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Submit a contact form

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide.

Use of Personal Information

We may use your personal information to:

  • Process reservations
  • Send marketing communications
  • Respond to inquiries

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose your personal information:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: To third-party service providers engaged by the restaurant for purposes such as reservation management, payment processing, or marketing services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required or compelled by law enforcement, government agencies, or legal processes, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.
  • Safety and Security: If necessary to protect the safety and security of our customers, employees, or the public in response to safety concerns or security threats.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or other business transition, personal information may be disclosed as part of the transfer of assets. Customers will be notified about such transfers and any changes to the privacy policy.
  • Consent: With your explicit consent or at your direction for a specific purpose, such as sharing your contact information with a partner restaurant for a joint promotional event.

Cookies and Analytics

In addition to the above, we also use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our website and hold certain information. Our website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website. This information is used to compile reports and to help us improve the website.

Please note that Google Analytics may collect your IP address, which is considered personal information under certain jurisdictions. However, we do not have access to this information, as it is collected and processed by Google Analytics in accordance with their privacy policy.

You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@cafenorwood.com.
