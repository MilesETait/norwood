FAQs

How much notice is required for catering orders?

We require two business days' notice for individual meal packs and shared plates, and three business days' notice for charcuterie boards.

What is the minimum order?

Our individual meal packs require a minimum order of six per option.

Can I customize the menu?

Absolutely! We offer customized menus for both catering and event hires. Contact us for more details.

Do you offer vegetarian or gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian and gluten-free options across our catering packages.
